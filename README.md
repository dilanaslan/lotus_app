# Lotus App 🪷
> **A Digital Mental Health Support Application for University Students**

Lotus App is a multi-modal, privacy-first mobile/web application designed to help university students monitor their emotional well-being, track stress levels, and access instant, low-barrier AI support—especially during high-stress academic periods. 

Developed as a Computer Engineering Senior Design Project (CENG 402).

---

## 🚀 Key Features

- **Empathetic AI Chatbot:** Driven by a hybrid retrieval-generation pipeline providing context-aware, safe, and supportive text interactions.
- **Structured Emotional Monitoring:** An intuitive mood tracker combined with an emotional self-assessment slider to help students quickly capture their daily state.
- **Personalized Journaling:** A secure reflection space that integrates directly with the chatbot to offer highly contextualized conversational support.
- **Safety & Crisis Detection:** Embedded safety filters designed to detect acute distress and safely guide users toward professional university support resources.
